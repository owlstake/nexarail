# Rejected or Waitlisted Validator Responses — NexaRail Testnet

**Chain:** nexarail-testnet-1
**Phase:** 7D — Application Review
**Audience:** Coordinator (internal) — templates for communicating decisions

---

## Overview

Not all validator applications will be accepted. This document provides standardised responses for rejected and waitlisted applicants. Every response must be professional, clear, and honest.

**All responses must reinforce:**
- This is a testnet-only decision
- No financial judgment or value assessment is implied
- Rejection is not permanent — applicants may reapply
- The controlled registration cap is limited

---

## Rejection Reasons (Select One)

### R1: Hardware Below Minimum

The current minimum hardware requirement for NexaRail testnet validators is:
- 4 vCPU
- 8 GB RAM
- 100 GB SSD
- Linux host (Ubuntu 22.04+ or equivalent)
- Static public IP

Your described infrastructure did not meet one or more of these requirements. If your infrastructure changes, you are welcome to reapply.

### R2: No Linux Host

NexaRail testnet validators must run on **Linux hosts**. Based on your application, your intended infrastructure does not meet this requirement.

**Docker Desktop on macOS is not supported** for validator operation due to P2P networking instability identified during rehearsal testing.

If you can provision a Linux host (cloud VPS or bare metal), you are welcome to reapply.

### R3: Insufficient Validator Experience

At this stage of the controlled testnet, we are prioritising operators with demonstrated validator experience — particularly on Cosmos SDK or Tendermint-based chains.

This is not a reflection on your technical capability. As the testnet matures and we expand the validator set, experience requirements may be relaxed. You are welcome to reapply.

### R4: Application Incomplete

Your application was missing required information that prevented us from completing our review. Common missing items include:
- Contact information
- Infrastructure details
- Validator experience
- Acknowledgement of testnet-only disclaimers

Please submit a complete application to be reconsidered.

### R5: Incompatible with Testnet-Only Nature

The NexaRail testnet is strictly for infrastructure testing. We are unable to accept validators who characterise participation as an investment, assert that testnet tokens have monetary value, or make claims about token sales or financial returns.

These claims are inconsistent with the testnet's nature and purpose. If you can acknowledge the testnet-only, no-value status, you are welcome to reapply.

### R6: Jurisdiction

After review, we are unable to accept validator applications from your stated jurisdiction for this intake round. This is a precautionary decision based on the controlled nature of this testnet.

This decision may change in future rounds. You are welcome to reapply.

### R7: Impersonation or Fraud

[Manual response — no template. Assess situation individually. May include permanent exclusion.]

### R8: Capacity Reached (Waitlisted)

The controlled validator registration for this intake round has reached its target capacity. Your application met our requirements, but no slots are currently available.

You have been placed on the **waitlist**. If a slot becomes available, you will be contacted. We expect to review capacity periodically.

## Follow-Up

- Rejected applicants: No further contact unless they reapply
- Waitlisted applicants: Notify if slot opens (monthly check)
- Do not debate or negotiate rejection decisions
- Escalate appeals to coordinator for review

---

## Notes for Coordinator

- Personalise each response — don't just copy-paste
- Be kind but direct. "Not accepted" is clearer than "unfortunately we cannot..."
- Never promise future acceptance
- Never imply that mainnet slots are reserved
- Never discuss token value, price, or investment
- If an applicant becomes hostile: stop responding, document, escalate
- Archive all rejection communications for 12 months
